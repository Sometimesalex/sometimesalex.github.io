Project Brief

Project 67 is an experimental multimedia project combining original music, interactive video frameworks, and adaptive audiovisual structures. Users influence the content through interaction, earning tokens that unlock new ways to participate. The system demonstrates the intersection of creativity, adaptability, and user identity, offering a novel form of participatory media.

This work is inspired by the broader concept of "Proof of Experience" (PoE), a human-centric alternative to traditional proof-of-work systems described in the Proof-of-Experience Crypto repository. PoE reframes how digital participation and value can be established by leveraging lived experience rather than specialized hardware, aligning with Project 67’s emphasis on user engagement and identity.

This site serves as the central hub for the project while the full interactive platform is under development. It presents existing audio, planned visual interactivity, and an overview of future capabilities.
